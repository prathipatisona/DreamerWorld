Loan Amount

Duration

Number of EMIs you will have to pay for paying down the loan, we calculate and provide a plan list.

EMI Amount

This is the amount which you will need to pay every month.Try our loan EMI calculator to find the amount you need to pay as EMI for your loan.

First EMI Date

This is the date on which the first EMI payment starts. You will be required to pay the EMI amount on this day every month, including the first EMI date.

Interest

This is the total interest which you would pay on the loan. This is effectively the interest cost you will pay for getting the loan amount. It can be calculated as (EMI Amount * Duration - Loan Amount).

Processing Fee

This is the processing fee which is to be paid by you for availing the loan. Dreamers World charges a processing fee of 3%, with a minimum amount of Rs. 500/-
